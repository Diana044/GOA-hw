Discover Kakheti

The Heart of Wine, Nature, and Tradition in Georgia

Welcome to Kakheti

Kakheti is one of the most beautiful regions of Georgia, known for its rich culture, ancient history, and world-famous wine production. Nestled in the fertile valleys of the Caucasus Mountains, Kakheti offers stunning landscapes, historical monuments, and unique traditions that have shaped its identity.

The Wine Region

Georgia, and particularly Kakheti, is often called the "Cradle of Wine." The region is home to some of the oldest wine-making traditions in the world, dating back over 8,000 years. The warm climate and fertile soil create ideal conditions for growing unique grape varieties, producing wines that are sought after globally.

Explore Kakheti

From ancient monasteries to breathtaking nature reserves, Kakheti offers something for everyone. Whether you're exploring the historical sites like the Alaverdi Monastery or hiking through the wine-producing valleys, Kakheti is a paradise for adventurers and culture enthusiasts alike.

Traditional Music of Kakheti

The music of Kakheti is deeply rooted in the traditions and rituals of the region. Georgian polyphonic singing is a UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage, and Kakheti is home to some of the most iconic performances. The unique sounds reflect the connection between the people and their land.
