Anthony Edwards

Anthony Edwards is an American professional basketball player for the Minnesota Timberwolves in the NBA. Born on August 5, 2001, in Atlanta, Georgia, he played college basketball at the University of Georgia before being selected as the first overall pick in the 2020 NBA Draft. Known for his explosive athleticism, scoring ability, and charismatic personality, Edwards has quickly become one of the league's brightest young stars. A versatile shooting guard/small forward, he excels in driving to the basket, three-point shooting, and highlight-reel dunks.

"I’m never satisfied. I’m always hungry. I’m always working to get better."
