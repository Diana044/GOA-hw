Tesla Model S

The Future of Electric Luxury

Introduction

The Tesla Model S is an all-electric luxury sedan that has set the standard for electric vehicles worldwide. With cutting-edge technology, superior performance, and unmatched efficiency, the Model S is a symbol of Tesla's commitment to sustainable energy and innovation.

Key Features

  • Acceleration: 0 to 60 mph in under 2 seconds (Plaid model).
  • Range: Up to 405 miles on a single charge.
  • Autopilot: Advanced driver assistance system with Full Self-Driving capabilities.
  • Interior: Premium materials, large touchscreen, and spacious design.
  • Charging: Supercharger network with fast-charging capabilities.
